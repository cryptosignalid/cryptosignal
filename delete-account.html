Delete Your Account

We respect your right to remove your data from our platform at any time, permanently.

Permanent Action — Cannot Be Undone. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data — including alert preferences, subscription history, and saved signal settings — from our systems within 30 days of confirmation.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account credentials and email address
  • Saved alert configurations and notification preferences
  • Signal history and watchlist settings
  • Pro subscription records (subscription cancelled first)

How to Delete Your Account

1
Cancel Active Subscription
If you have an active Pro subscription, please cancel it first to avoid further charges. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan within the app, or include a cancellation request in your email to us and we will process it on your behalf.
2
Send a Deletion Request by Email
Send an email to privacy@cryptosignal.id with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

Include in the body: the email address associated with your account, and optionally a brief reason for deletion (helps us improve the product). REQUIRED STEP
3
Receive Confirmation
We will send a confirmation email within 3 business days. Your account and all associated data will be fully purged from our systems within 30 days of the confirmed request.
Account Deletion Contact

Confirmation: Within 3 business days
Full data removal: Within 30 days of confirmation
