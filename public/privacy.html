CryptoSignal.id ("we", "our", "us") operates the website https://cryptosignal.id and its associated mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

By using CryptoSignal.id, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — when you sign up for free signal alerts or upgrade to Pro.
  • Telegram Chat ID — if you choose to connect Telegram for push alerts (optional).
  • Payment information — processed securely by DOKU (our payment provider). We never store your card numbers.

Information collected automatically:

  • Anonymous usage events (page views, signal clicks, filter changes) — no personal identifiers attached.
  • Device type and browser (for compatibility purposes only).
  • IP address (for rate limiting and fraud prevention; not stored long-term).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver real-time crypto signal alerts via in-app feed and Telegram.
  • To process and verify Pro subscription payments.
  • To send you transactional emails (payment receipts, Pro activation).
  • To improve signal accuracy and product quality using aggregated, anonymous analytics.
  • To communicate product updates if you have opted in.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We share data only with:

  • DOKU — payment processing. Subject to their Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram — if you connect your Telegram account for alerts.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider. Infrastructure only, no data access.
  • Upstash (Redis) — stores subscriber status and alert preferences.

We may disclose information if required by Indonesian law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

CryptoSignal.id uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Your Pro status after verification.
  • Your alert preferences (coin watchlist, impact thresholds).
  • Your email address after capture (to avoid re-prompting).

This data is stored locally on your device only. You can clear it via your browser settings at any time.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

5. Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by DOKU, a licensed Indonesian payment gateway. When you make a payment:

  • Card and virtual account details are entered directly on DOKU's secure pages.
  • CryptoSignal.id receives only a payment confirmation (order ID, status, email).
  • We store your email and subscription expiry date in our database to verify Pro access.

6. Telegram Integration

If you connect Telegram alerts (Pro feature):

  • You provide your Telegram Chat ID to us.
  • We use this only to send you crypto signal notifications you have configured.
  • You can disconnect Telegram at any time via the Alerts page, which removes your Chat ID from our system.

7. Data Retention

  • Subscriber records — retained for 12 months after subscription expiry, then deleted.
  • Email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Anonymous analytics — aggregated, retained for 90 days.
  • Telegram Chat IDs — deleted immediately upon disconnection request.

8. Your Rights

Under Indonesian data protection law (UU PDP No. 27 Tahun 2022), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing emails at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@cryptosignal.id. We respond within 14 business days.

9. Children's Privacy

CryptoSignal.id is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests:

  • Email: privacy@cryptosignal.id
  • Support: support@cryptosignal.id
  • Website: https://cryptosignal.id
