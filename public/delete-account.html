Delete Your Account

We respect your right to remove your data from our platform at any time.

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. All your data — including alert preferences, subscription history, and saved signals — will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

What Will Be Deleted

  1. Your account credentials and email address
  2. Saved alert configurations and notification preferences
  3. Signal history and watchlist settings
  4. Pro subscription records (cancellation will be processed first)

How to Delete Your Account

1 Cancel Active Subscription

If you have an active Pro subscription, please cancel it first. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan within the app, or contact us to cancel on your behalf.

2 Send Deletion Request

Send an email to privacy@cryptosignal.id with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account.

3 Confirmation

We will send a confirmation email within 3 business days. Your data will be fully purged from all our systems within 30 days of the confirmed request.

Contact for Account Deletion

Email: privacy@cryptosignal.id
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Response time: Within 3 business days
Full data removal: Within 30 days of confirmation
